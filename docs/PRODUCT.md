# RailYatra Product Specification

## 🎯 Product Vision
RailYatra is not simply a railway booking utility. It is **the AI Operating System for Travel Decisions**. 

People don't want tickets; they want **certainty**. The product aims to solve uncertainty before, during, and after railway travel. The AI operates as a complete railway travel assistant available 24/7.

---

## 👥 Target Users
- **Daily Commuters:** Prioritize delay prediction, platform changes, and coach layouts.
- **Families & Seniors:** Require comfort optimization, lower berth recommendation, boarding support, and food arrangements.
- **Business Travelers:** Require automated expense exports, receipts, and fast alternative schedules.
- **Pilgrims & Tourists:** Need multi-modal itineraries, local transit links, and station facility info.

---

## 🎨 Design Philosophy
The system provides Apple-level polish, Stripe-level simplicity, and Linear-level animations.
- **Premium:** Every interaction must feel expensive.
- **Minimal:** Not flashy, not cluttered. Fast, clean, modern, and elegant.
- **Adaptive:** Support for premium Dark Mode, Light Mode, Auto Mode, and High Contrast.

---

## 💰 Monetization Model

### 1. Free Tier
- 3 AI Journey Analyses / month.
- Basic PNR status tracking.
- Train and station search.
- Delay predictions.

### 2. Premium Tier (Monthly/Annual Subscription)
- Unlimited AI chat and voice assistant support.
- Optimization agents (Boarding Station, Date, Class, and Fare).
- Advanced travel reports and smart alerts.
- Calendar synchronization.

### 3. Premium+ Tier (Corporate & Multi-user)
- Multi-user team dashboard.
- Consolidated business travel expense reports.
- API access keys.
- AI Concierge assistance.
